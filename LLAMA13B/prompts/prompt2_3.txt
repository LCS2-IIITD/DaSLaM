question: Weng earns $12 an hour for babysitting. Yesterday, she just did 50 minutes of babysitting. How much did she earn yesterday?

Solution: Let us think step by step, 
1. Money per hour for babysititng is $12
2. Number of minutes babysitting done is 50
3. Number of minutes in an hour is 60
4. Number of hours babysitting done, which is 50/60 = 0.833
5. Money earned is number of hours babysit * money per hour for babysit, which is 0.833 * $12 = $10
6. We are asked money earned, which is $10
7. Answer: $10 ###

question: Albert is wondering how much pizza he can eat in one day. He buys 2 large pizzas and 2 small pizzas. A large pizza has 16 slices and a small pizza has 8 slices. If he finishes everything, how many slices combined?

Solution: Let us think step by step, 
1. Large pizzas bought is 2
2. Small pizzas bought is 2
3. Slices in small pizza is 8
4. Slices in large pizza is 16
5. Total slices in small pizza is 2*8 = 16
6. Total slices in large pizza is 2*16 = 32
7. Slices in total = slices in large pizza+ slices in small pizza = 16 + 32 = 48
8. We are asked slices in total, which is 48
9. Answer: 48 ###
